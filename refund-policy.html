Our Credit-Based Refund System

Instead of cash refunds, we offer a unique credit system to ensure commitment to your wellness journey while providing flexibility.

₹99 Session Credit Details

Your discovery call payment converts to redeemable credit when:

  • You attend your scheduled session
  • The session is completed in full
  • Credit issued within 24 hours post-session

Credit Redemption

  • Applicable to: Any paid yoga plan (₹899/month or higher)
  • Validity: 7 days from issue date
  • Transfer: Non-transferable to other clients
  • Stacking: May be combined with promotional offers

Non-Credit Scenarios

₹99 will not convert to credit if:

  • You don't attend the scheduled session
  • You arrive more than 10 minutes late
  • Technical issues prevent completion (without rescheduling)
  • You cancel with less than 24 hours notice

Special Circumstances

  • Medical Emergencies: Documentation required for credit extension
  • Technical Failures: Rescheduled sessions retain credit eligibility
  • Service Issues: Credit refunded if we fail to deliver service

How to Redeem Credit

  1. Complete your discovery call
  2. Receive unique coupon code via email
  3. Apply code at checkout for any paid plan
  4. Discount automatically applied
