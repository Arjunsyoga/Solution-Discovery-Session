Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-jul-2025 | Last Updated: 16-jul-2025

At AryaFlow & ArjunsYoga ("we", "us"), we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information about participants in our yoga discovery call program.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a discovery call, we collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number
  • Health Information: Primary concerns, duration of issues, discomfort level
  • Payment Information: Transaction details via Razorpay (we don't store card details)
  • Session Data: Notes from our consultation

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule and conduct your discovery call
  • Personalize your yoga recommendations
  • Process your ₹99 payment and issue session credits
  • Send relevant follow-up information
  • Improve our services (aggregated data only)

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure storage in password-protected systems
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Scheduling Tools: Calendly for appointment management
  • Legal Compliance: When required by Indian law

Your Data Rights

Under Indian IT Act, you have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • For active clients: Until 6 months after last contact
  • Transaction records: 5 years for tax compliance
  • Health notes: 3 years for service continuity
